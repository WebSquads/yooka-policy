Yooka – Community & Safety Standards

Age Restriction:
Yooka is strictly for users aged 18 years and above. Access is not allowed for minors. Users must confirm their age during registration.

Chat and Voice:
Yooka allows users to communicate via in-app text chat and real-time voice chat in public or private rooms. No audio chat is stored on our servers. We prioritize real-time interaction only.

Safety and Moderation:
Users must not engage in harassment, nudity, hate speech, bullying, or any form of illegal activity. Such behavior is strictly prohibited and may lead to immediate ban or suspension.

Reporting and Blocking:
Users can block or report others directly from within the app. Reported behavior is reviewed promptly by our moderation team.

User Verification:
We use manual and automated measures to discourage underage users and fake accounts, and monitor behavior patterns to detect policy violations.

Privacy:
We do not store user voice chat. Usernames, profile pictures, and other basic info are stored securely on our servers and never shared with third parties without user consent.
